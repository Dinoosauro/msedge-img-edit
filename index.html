Privacy

msedge-img-edit doesn't send any of your images or any other data to a server.

All of the data is elaborated locally. Hosting is provided by GitHub pages, and, to make this page a little decent-looking, a font (Work Sans) is downloaded from Google Fonts' servers. msedge-img-edit doesn't share anything with them.

msedge-img-edit saves in the website's isolated local storage the theme the user selected. This is the only data msedge-img-edit saves on your device, and you can delete it when you want from your browser's settings.
